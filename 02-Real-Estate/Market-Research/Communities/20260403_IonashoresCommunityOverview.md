## COMMUNITY OVERVIEW
Iona Shores is a **premier waterfront community** located in South Fort Myers, Florida. This prestigious residential area is known for its direct, bridge-free sailboat access to the Caloosahatchee River and the Gulf of Mexico. The community offers an exceptional lifestyle for boating enthusiasts, waterfront living seekers, and those looking for a quiet, upscale residential experience.
**Key Tagline:** "The Nice Side of Fort Myers" - Closest to the Beach
---
## LOCATION & GEOGRAPHY
### Geographic Details
- **County:** Lee County, Florida
- **Region:** Southwest Florida, South Fort Myers
- **Proximity to Water:** Directly adjacent to Caloosahatchee River with Gulf of Mexico access
- **Area:** 10.23 square miles
- **Elevation:** 7 feet (2.1 m)
- **Census Designation:** Unincorporated community & Census-Designated Place (CDP)
### Accessibility & Distance
- **From Sanibel Island:** Approximately 5 miles
- **From Fort Myers Beach:** Close proximity (within 10-15 minutes)
- **From Downtown Fort Myers:** Approximately 33 minutes by car
- **To Sanibel Causeway:** Within 5 miles - easy beach access
- **To Gulf of Mexico:** Direct water access
### Geographic Setting
Located off Bain Road near Iona Road, Iona Shores is situated within the **Iona census-designated place** and is part of the **Cape Coral-Fort Myers Metropolitan Statistical Area**. The community is nestled along the **Caloosahatchee River** in one of Southwest Florida's most desirable waterfront locations.

## HISTORY & DEVELOPMENT
### Historical Background
- Iona Shores has **deep roots in Florida's cattle industry**, with historical connections to early ranching operations in the region
- The area is named after **McGregor Boulevard**, which honors the historical legacy of the region
- Originally developed as a residential waterfront community focused on single-family homes and waterfront estates
- **Development Era:** Primarily 1960s-present, with homes ranging from 1960s bungalows to large modern waterfront estates
### Development Character
- **Non-gated community** with a mix of waterfront and inland residential properties
- Primarily single-family homes, though some multi-family properties exist
- Features numerous canals and waterfront properties with direct Gulf access

## HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION (HOA)
### HOA Structure
- **Status:** Mixed HOA - some subdivisions have HOA, some are non-gated without mandatory HOA
- **Similar Community Note:** Iona Gardens (nearby) is known for being a community **without mandatory HOA** - some buyers prefer this freedom
- **Neighboring HOAs:** Several HOAs operate in surrounding areas:
  - Colonial Shores (Rated 4.8★, gated community)
  - Botanica Lakes Homeowners Association (Rated 4.3★)
  - Various other associations in the broader Iona/Fort Myers area
### HOA Considerations
- **Community Amenities:** Vary by subdivision - may include boat docks, waterfront access, marina facilities
- **HOA Fees:** Vary by property type (typically $50-$150/month depending on amenities and location)
- **Governance:** Property owners have voting rights and input on community matters
- **Maintenance:** Generally well-maintained community
### Important Note for Buyers
Some buyers specifically seek properties in **non-gated communities with optional HOA** (like certain Iona Shores subdivisions) for:
- Greater freedom in property modifications
- Lower mandatory fees
- Less restrictive covenant regulations
- More flexible lifestyle
